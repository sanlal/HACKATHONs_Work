# Screenshot checklist

Capture PNG images at 1440×900 or a similar 16:10 desktop size. Use fictional
data and hide browser bookmarks, emails and credentials.

Store final images under `public/screenshots/`.

1. `01-home-hero.png`
   - Route: `/`
   - Show the name, elevator pitch and all three pillars.

2. `02-work-ai-listing.png`
   - Route: `/work`
   - Employer view with GPT-5.6 suggestions and the **Live GPT-5.6** badge.

3. `03-work-match-explanation.png`
   - Route: `/work`
   - Deterministic match score and explanation.

4. `04-produce-offers.png`
   - Route: `/produce`
   - Two bids, calculated totals, pickup dates and benchmark disclaimer.

5. `05-books-donation.png`
   - Route: `/books`
   - Free donation listing and transparent learner requests.

6. `06-telugu.png`
   - Route: `/work` or `/produce`
   - Telugu navigation and Telugu AI assistance.

7. `07-dashboard-trust.png`
   - Route: `/dashboard`
   - Live mode badge, capabilities, notifications and activity-earned trust.

## Devpost use

- Use `01-home-hero.png` as the first gallery image.
- Use the generated Open Graph image for social previews.
- Keep captions outcome-focused and avoid unsupported claims such as government
  verification, guaranteed prices or escrow.
